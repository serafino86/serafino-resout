## Value logic

- The free diagnostic removes risk for the client: they already receive value before paying anything
- The 300 CHF plan is useful even if the mission stops there - it is not a trapped funnel
- Implementation starts from 800 CHF per standalone project, paid once. It is not a monthly subscription by default.
- Observed average ticket: 2,800 to 3,800 CHF per complete mission
- The delivered tool belongs to the client, with no subscription or permanent dependency

## Guarantee

**Satisfied or refunded within 3 months.** If within 3 months after implementation, following the agreed usage protocol, no measurable result is observed — the client is fully refunded.

## Frequent pricing questions

**Is 800 CHF for a project a lot?**
Compared with one hour of classic consulting (200-300 CHF), no. And unlike a consultant, Serafino delivers a tool that keeps working after the mission ends. The exact price depends on the scope.

**Are there hidden costs?**
No. Serafino works in the Google ecosystem the client already has. There is no software to buy, no server to rent, no additional subscription.

**Can the client stop after the 300 CHF plan?**
Yes, absolutely. The plan has value by itself. Many clients use it as a roadmap they implement themselves.
