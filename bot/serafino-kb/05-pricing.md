## What each step means in practice

### 0 CHF — Diagnostic (45 minutes)
Not a sales call. Enrico looks at how the business actually works, identifies where time and money are being lost, and gives an honest answer: either there is something concrete to do, or there isn't. No commitment either way.

### 300 CHF — Simplification plan
A structured PDF with: the 3 identified bottlenecks, concrete actions to fix them, success indicators. Useful even if the client implements it themselves.

### 800 CHF — Light setup
For smaller businesses. Usually Google Workspace configuration, automations, basic dashboard. No server, no bot.

### 2 500 CHF — Centralina Standard
The main product. Deployed in 24 hours. 2 weeks of fixing included. 3 months of accompaniment to autonomy included. See file 10-centralina.md for full details.

### 4 500–6 000 CHF — Centralina Pro
Same as Standard with advanced modules specific to HORECA or complex multi-team businesses.

### 300–800 CHF — Brain Updates
Client already has Centralina or another delivered system. Wants to add a module, integrate a new tool, expand to a new use case.

## Common pricing questions

**"Is 2 500 CHF a lot?"**
A secretary costs more per month. Centralina works 24/7, never calls in sick, and belongs to you. Compare with: 10–15 hours of manual work per week × 12 months. How much is that time worth?

**"Are there hidden costs after?"**
Hosting: 100 CHF/year if you use Serafino's server (optional). Nothing else. No subscriptions.

**"Can I start with just the diagnostic?"**
Yes. Free, no commitment. Most clients who do the diagnostic find at least one real problem worth solving.

**"Can I stop after the 300 CHF plan?"**
Yes. Many clients implement parts of it themselves. The plan has standalone value.

**"What if it doesn't work?"**
3-month accompaniment included. The system adapts to the real workflow during those 3 months. After that, if no measurable result was achieved following the agreed protocol — full refund.
